Jiu Jitsu Is Chess

http://jiujitsuischess.com

A blog I started for grappling athletes to make the most of their training time based on my personal experiences as a part-time jiu-jitsu competitor. I share the ideas and principles behind my competitive success as a grappling athlete and explain how the average grappler can implement them into their own training and competitive career.

The founding principle behind Jiu-Jitsu Is Chess is that utilizing principles of efficiency is the key to maximizing the returns of an athlete's training time and reaching one's potential, something that I've found holds true in nearly any competitive endeavor one can devote themselves to, be it athletics, entrepreneurship, or programming.
